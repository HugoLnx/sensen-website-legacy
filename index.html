Brothers and Gamers

You will be playing the games of two brothers that played together countless games on Super Nintendo, Playstation 1 and Playstation 2. Now we hope to bring to our games, the joy we felt playing the two genres we like the most: Platformers and Fighting Games.

Hard, Intense and Concise Games

Those are the three main principles of our games!

  • Hard: It will be fair, but... seriously... don't blame us if you cry.
  • Intense: Simple story, no much talking, you'll get to the action!
  • Concise: Life is short, no grinding, you will just beat the bad guys.

Inspirations

The games we love playing and inspire us are: Super Ghouls'n Ghosts, Megaman X, Megaman X4 Zero, Super Smash Bros, Celeste, The Messenger, Street Fighter, Marvel vs Capcom, Castlevania Symphony of the Night, and others.
